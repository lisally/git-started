SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is the protagonist of the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants. He is depicted as a cheerful and naive yellow sea sponge living under the sea. He lives in a pineapple shaped house with his pet snail Gary in a city called Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob works as a cook at a fast food restaurant named Krusty Krab. His best friend is a starfish named Patrick who often joins SpongeBob on his various adventures.
