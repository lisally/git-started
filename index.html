SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a cartoon character that is depicted as a cheerful and optimistic yellow sea sponge. He lives under the sea in a city called Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob resides in a pineapple house with his pet snail, Gary. He works as a chef at a fast food restaurant named the Krusty Krab. His best friend is a starfish named Patrick who often joins SpongeBob on his various adventures.

SpongeBob SquarePants is the protagonist of the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants.
